What to Expect?
Welcome to Kairos, our church's youth group designed exclusively for ages 13-17. Experience the ultimate gathering of young minds where faith, fun, and friendship intertwine seamlessly.

From engaging discussions to exciting events, Kairos is the prime avenue for vibrant fellowship and personal growth. Join us for an unforgettable journey of camaraderie and discovery.
Why Kairos
Imagine a space where laughter echoes through the halls, where friendships are forged through shared experiences, and where faith is explored in a modern and relatable way. Kairos offers a unique blend of engaging activities, interactive discussions, and exciting events that make every gathering a memorable adventure.

Join us at Kairos and discover a place where you can be yourself, build lifelong connections, and deepen your understanding of faith—all while having an incredible time. Your journey of fun, fellowship, and personal growth starts here.

YouthGiG Cafe Broadcast every thursday on Facebook and Spotify

Embrace adventure and growth at our yearly Youth Camp! A week of friends, fun, and self-discovery through challenges, talks, and worship. Elevate your journey with us!

Welcome to Youth Sunday – a vibrant monthly tradition where the future leads the way! On the last Sunday of each month, our youth take center stage, infusing energy and fresh perspectives into our service.
