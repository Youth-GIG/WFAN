Kingdom kids Ministry
The Kingdom Kids Ministry is the children’s ministry of Word for All Nations. Our focus is to make Jesus known to children ages 3 to 12 years and to teach them in the ways of God.

We do this by following the mandate of Proverbs 22:6 to train them while they are young to be grounded in the Word of God, to establish their relationship with Jesus, to know their identity in Christ, and to become strong in the Spirit of God. And as they grow older, they stay on the path of forming their godly generation.
